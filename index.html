F/02 The problem

Decisions worth millions ride on data trusted by signature, not by proof.

Drillhole data is scattered across incompatible vendor formats, multiple labs, and decades of legacy files. At a raise, a listing, or a sale, an independent geologist has to personally certify, under legal liability, that it is verified. Today that proof takes weeks by hand, and everyone downstream takes it on faith.
